MISSION STATEMENT

In Defense of Animals' mission is to create a non-violent future for human and non-human animals, where both can live a natural and harmonious existence.

We work with community groups, educational organizations, and within the courts to defend animal health, welfare, and habitats around the world.

IDA also cares for lost, orphaned, abandoned, and abused animals in 3 sanctuaries around the world: Hope Animal Sanctuary in rural Mississippi, IDA India in Mumbai, India, and IDA Africa in Cameroon

IDA leads the way in compassionate strategic activism by teaching approaches towards peaceful transformation within each person, within IDA, within the animal-rights movement, and within the world. We work together to provide a higher image of human identity and animal sentience.

We believe in the words of Martin Luther King Jr.:

"Whatever affects on directly, affects all indirectly. I can never be what I ought to be until you are what you ought to be. This is the interrelated structure of reality."
