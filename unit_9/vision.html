VISION - WHERE WE ARE HEADED

A world transformed by awareness of our true human destiny and the inate potential of every living creature. Where life is sacred in all its forms and where human systems are in harmony with nature and foster and sustain that life.

We envision a world where humans value all life, regardless of species. A world devoid of slaughterhouses and factory farms. A world without poaching and the exotic pet trade. A world where every pet has a home and animal abuse is part of a history best forgotten. We envision a world where unharmed peacemaking replaces military intervention. Where restorative justice replaces retribution. Where the economies of needs are based on simplicity and harmony for the environment.

Our vision is based on simple propositions:

  1. Humans are a part of the natural world, and all life is interconnected
  2. Indefinite consumptions cannot fulfill us but expanding relationships will
  3. The human potential for compassion and empathy has only just begun to be understood
  4. The humane and respectful treatment of all animal life leads to less conflict and greater respect among all humans
